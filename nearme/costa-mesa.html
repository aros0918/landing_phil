About Costa Mesa, CA

Costa Mesa, CA, is a mid-sized city located in Orange County with a population of approximately 112,148 people and a population density of 7,114 people per square mile. The city's median age is 36, and the population is diverse, with 49.4% White, 8.6% Asian, and 36.5% Hispanic residents.

Costa Mesa is known for its thriving suburban environment and is situated just one mile from the Pacific Ocean. The city offers a variety of neighborhoods, each with its own unique character, such as the College Park District, Mesa North, and South Coast Metro. Costa Mesa has a rich history, with Native American roots and the establishment of Mission San Juan Capistrano in the area.

The city's real estate market is diverse, and it is a major commercial and industrial center. Costa Mesa is also a hub for art and culture, home to institutions like the Tony-winning theatre South Coast Repertory and the Segerstrom Center for the Arts. Additionally, the city hosts various events throughout the year, including Concerts in the Park and the Orange County Fair, making it a vibrant and engaging place to live
