About Buena Park, CA

Buena Park is a city located in northern Orange County, California, with a population of approximately 83,000 people. The city is known for its diverse culture and vibrant community, offering a mix of suburban and urban lifestyles.

Buena Park is home to several popular attractions, including Knott's Berry Farm, a renowned theme park with rides, shows, and a water park, as well as the interactive entertainment center, Knott's Soak City. Additionally, the city features the Medieval Times Dinner & Tournament, offering visitors a unique dining and entertainment experience. Buena Park also hosts the annual Silverado Days festival, celebrating the city's heritage with live music, food, and carnival rides.

The city's cultural diversity is reflected in its dining scene, with a wide range of international cuisines available. Overall, Buena Park offers a dynamic blend of entertainment, cultural experiences, and community events, making it a vibrant and engaging place to visit or reside.
